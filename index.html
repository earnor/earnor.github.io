Biography

As a research assistant at ETH Zürich, I concentrate on infrastructure planning, providing planning decision support for decisions made 10-30 years before any infrastructure development is put into operation. At this point in time, decisions are made considering a high degree of future uncertainty. The research contributes analysis of processes, governance structure and novel tools to make robust planning decisions bearing in mind uncertain land-use transport interactions.
